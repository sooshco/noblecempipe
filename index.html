Product

Product

AC Pressure Pipes- Asbestos cement pipes are made of a mixture of asbestos paste and cement compressed by steel rollers to form a laminated material of great strength and density. When it comes to heavy duty usage AC Pressure Pipes are an ideal choice.They are apt for irrigation and casing purposes. We adhere to the highest international standards while preparing them.

Features ->

Great water Carrying Capacity

AC pipes have become one of the best choices for portable constructions of the water mains.

Asbestos pipes incorporate relatively high amounts of durability as compared to the other cement pipes available in the market

Tapping and installation processes in case of AC pipes are relatively easy as compared to other pipes.

Available in different dimensions and customizable to customer needs.
